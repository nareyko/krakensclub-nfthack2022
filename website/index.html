  • Epic (~11% of NFTs)

NFT LEVELS

Each Krakens Sailing Club NFT has a special attribute/feature - a level . For the first mint only Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 1 will be issued.

Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 2 will be twice as rare as Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 1 on average (NFTs collections of each next level will be twice as rare on average as NFTs of the previous level).

To get Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 2, you can exchange two Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 1 (any rarity) to one random Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 2. Level 1 NFTs will be returned to the the Mystic portal pool (or to Krakens Sailing Club).

So, even in case of getting not very rare NFTs during the first mint, you can get a more exclusive NFT. Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 8 can not be used in the Mystic Portal.

Giveaways:

Mint:

Mystic portal (initial pool):

  • Level 2. 4096 NFTs. Rarity: Legendary-Mythic. Votes: 3/NFT
  • Level 3. 2048 NFTs. Rarity: Epic-Mythic. Votes: 9/NFT

Later NFTs levels will be tied to Krakens Sailing Club bonuses for offline events. The higher the NFTs level is, the more bonuses you will get.

Krakens Sailing Club team can mint additional NFTs of any level for any marketing and business purpose.

POWER PORTAL

If you have a very rare Krakens Sailing Club NFT, you can upgrade it to the next level.

You should have the second NFT of the same level. The first NFT, which you would like to upgrade will increase its level by 1.

The second NFT will be returned to the Mystic portal pool (or to Krakens Sailing Club).

Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 0 can not be used in the Power Portal.

SWAP PORTAL

If you have two Krakens Sailing Club NFTs with different levels you may just swap their levels using smart contract functionality.

Krakens Sailing Club NFTs level 0 can not be used in the Power Portal.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I own the Krakens Sailing Club NFT after purchasing?

You have broad rights to use the art associated with your NFT under Krakens Sailing Club NFT License. You can do any of the following:

  • Use the art for your own personal, non-commercial use;
  • Use the art when you’re on a marketplace that allows the purchase and sale of your art, as long as the marketplace cryptographically verifies that you are the owner;
  • Use the art when you’re on a third party website or app that allows the inclusion, involvement, or participation of your art, as long as the website/app cryptographically verifies that you are the owner, and the art doesn’t stay on the website/app after you’ve left;
  • Use the art to commercialize your own merchandise, provided that you aren’t earning more than $100,000 in revenue each year from doing so.

What can I do with my Krakens Sailing Club NFT?

You will be able to participate in issuing NFTs, exchanging current NFTs for new ones (see the description of the exchange), and upgrade your current NFTs.

Moreover, our team are looking forward to developing online and offline services and each Krakens Sailing Club NFT will be a clubcard, with VIP access to services developed.

How can I get Krakens Sailing Club NFT?

You can participate in minting during the drop date as well as buy NFTs from their current owners on different platforms.

There are no refunds for NFTs, and we will not field customer complaints. Please only buy NFTs if you understand that doing so does not necessarily give any direct economic value.

When is the drop? How much?

The nearest drop is planned for Q1 2022. 8192 NFTs level 1 will be minted.

Further mintings of Krakens Sailing Club NFTs will be available only for current Krakens Sailing Club NFTs owners.

Follow our socials and Discord to get the most up-to-date information.

Will there be a secondary market?

Yes, we are planning listing on one of the marketplaces, and we are currently negotiating with several of them.

In future we are going to create our own marketplace.

Are there secondary sale royalties? Will there be a community fund?

Yes. Initial royalties will be 10%.

We will be contributing 50% of the back from secondary market royalties into the community vault for buying back and 1 year blocking of the cheapest Krakens Sailing Club NFTs. After this, they will be returned for sale on the secondary market at market price.

We really value Krakens Sailing Club NFTs and club membership and this mechanism is aimed at NFTs deflation.

Which charities are being donated to?

We will donate a portion of our sales to nonprofits dealing with research of oceans and seas, and also to the projects aimed at continuous support of marine ecosystems and their inhabitants.

We will be donating funds to definite events, which reports and materials will be published on our site and in socials.
